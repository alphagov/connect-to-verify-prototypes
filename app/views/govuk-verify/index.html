Be sure your users are who they say they are

GOV.UK Verify is a trusted, secure way to prove identity. Designed to prevent fraud and protect users’ privacy, it’s a safe way to make sure you’re giving the right people access to your service.

Find out how to get started

GOV.UK Verify can:

  • help services save time and money processing face-to-face identity checks
  • help users save time waiting for documents to arrive in the post
  • replace physical signatures on legal documents

Find out if GOV.UK Verify is right for your service.

What GOV.UK Verify does

GOV.UK Verify:

  • lets users confirm their identity with personal data held by ‘certified companies’
  • helps services match individuals to records they already have or add new records if they need to
  • makes sure information isn’t stored in one place and is never shared unnecessarily
  • provides different levels of identity assurance so services can choose the right one for them
  • continually monitors for fraud

Read about how GOV.UK Verify works.

Secure by design

Working with the National Cyber Security Centre and the Privacy and Consumer Advisory Group, the GOV.UK Verify team designed GOV.UK Verify to:

  • meet EU rules like the general data protection regulation (GDPR)
  • meet the highest anti-fraud standards
  • protect users’ privacy

Read about the identity assurance principles that GOV.UK Verify follows.
