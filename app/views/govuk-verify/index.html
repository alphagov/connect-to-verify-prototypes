How it works

Users prove their identity to a private sector company who hold data about them and are trusted by government.

Your service then receives the user’s verified name, address and date of birth.

How it's being used

  • Verify reduces the time to access redundancy payments, to 3 days down from 3 months
  • Operator Licensing uses Verify to remove the need for a wet signature
  • DBS is making sure the right individuals are vetted using Verify
