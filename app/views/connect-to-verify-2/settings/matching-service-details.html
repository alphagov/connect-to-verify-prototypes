Matching service details

Verify call your matching service once the user has proved their identity.

You need to pick an entity id so Verify can uniquely refer to your matching service, and tell Verify a URL it can reach your matching service on.

What is this?

Your matching service entity id is a URL that Verify will use to uniquely identify your matching service. For example:

https://{your-service-name}.{your-integration-domain}/verify/matching-service

What is this?

Your matching service adapter matching URL is the URL used to receive Verify matching requests. For example

https://{your-integration-domain}/matching-service
